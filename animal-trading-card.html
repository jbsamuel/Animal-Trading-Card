Emperor Penguins

Emperor Penguins are one of the largest of all birds. They are also the biggest of 18 species of penguins

  • Scientifc Name: Aptenodytes forsteri
  • Average Height: 3.6 - 4.3 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 20-25 years
  • Habitat: Only found in Antartica

Recognizable by their large size and their tuxedo-like countershading, emperor penguins are the largest of all living penguin species. The max walking speed is 1.7mph, yet their swimming speed can reach 8.9mph (though it normally stays around 6.7mph).
